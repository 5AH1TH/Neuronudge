About Neuronudge

Neuronudge is designed to empower individuals with ADHD, Dyslexia, and general productivity challenges by offering smart task management tools tailored to your unique needs.

My mission is to provide an intuitive, accessible, and supportive environment to help you focus, organize, and achieve your goals with confidence.

Key Features

  • Personalized onboarding for ADHD, Dyslexia, and general profiles
  • AI-powered task prioritization and reminders
  • Easy task creation, editing, and progress tracking
  • Export tasks as JSON for backup or sharing
  • Responsive design for desktop and mobile
